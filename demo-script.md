# 🏆 NALS - Winning Demo Script

**Total Time:** 2 Minutes (Perfect for Hackathon Pitches)
**Goal:** High impact, zero fluff, pure storytelling.

---

## ⏱ 0–10 sec (THE HOOK)
*(Start on the Login Screen. Click "Access System".)*

**Speaker:**
"Students don’t fail because they don’t study — they fail because they forget."

---

## ⏱ 10–30 sec (THE PROBLEM)
*(Dashboard loads. Hover over the Cognitive Decay Curve.)*

**Speaker:**
"Current systems treat all learners the exact same. But memory decay is personal. If I study a database concept today, my brain forgets it at a completely different rate than yours."

---

## ⏱ 30–60 sec (THE SYSTEM)
*(Point to Global Retention, Decay Rate (λ), and the System Insight banner.)*

**Speaker:**
"This is NALS—the NeuroAdaptive Learning System. **What you’re seeing is not a dashboard — it’s a simulation of how your knowledge decays over time.** It models your exact memory decay using a personalized learning rate. Right now, I'm logged in as Alex. My retention is stable."

---

## ⏱ 60–90 sec (THE WOW MOMENT)
*(Click the user switcher at the top right: Switch from "Alex" to "Jordan".)*

**Speaker:**
"But watch what happens when we switch to Jordan. Same topic, different brain — completely different retention."

*(Pause for 1 second as the curve drops, pending revisions spike, and the insight banner changes.)*

"The entire AI engine recalculates instantly. Jordan's retention just plummeted. The system knows he's about to fail before he even takes a test."

---

## ⏱ 90–120 sec (THE KILLER FEATURE)
*(Scroll down to the AI Revision Planner. Click the dropdown arrow to reveal "Why this revision?" Then navigate to Analytics -> Cognitive Map.)*

**Speaker:**
"But we don't just give blind orders. We built Explainable AI. We show the exact math, the time gap, and the AI's confidence score. 

And if we look at the Cognitive Map... **we don’t just identify weak topics — we simulate how that weakness spreads across your knowledge graph.** If Jordan forgets 'Recursion', the system automatically flags 'Dynamic Programming' with a high risk score. It maps the cascading failure of human knowledge."

---

## ⏱ FINAL LINE (THE CLOSER)
*(Navigate back to Dashboard or stay on the Prediction vs Reality accuracy bar.)*

**Speaker:**
"Our prediction engine validates itself against actual quiz scores with 91.4% accuracy. 

NALS doesn’t help you study more — **it helps you forget less.** 

Thank you."
