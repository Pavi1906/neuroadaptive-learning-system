## Slide 2: The Problem (The "Why")
**The Flaw in Modern EdTech**
- **Static Algorithms:** Every student gets the same spaced repetition (e.g., Anki).
- **Isolated Knowledge:** Apps treat "Math" and "Physics" as separate, ignoring how forgetting one breaks the other.
- **Black Box AI:** Students are told *what* to study, but never *why*.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution (The "What")
**NALS: A Mathematical Mirror of Your Brain**
- **Personalized Decay Rate (λ):** We modified the Ebbinghaus Forgetting Curve to adapt to each user's unique learning velocity.
- **Cognitive Dependency Mapping:** We track how forgetting a foundational topic cascades into advanced topics.
- **Explainable AI:** We show the math behind every recommendation.

---

## Slide 4: The Core Innovation (The "How")
**The NALS Engine Architecture**
- *Visual:* A simple diagram showing:
  `Quiz Performance` -> `Updates User λ` -> `Recalculates Retention Curve` -> `Triggers Revision Plan`
- **The Math:** `Retention = e^(-λ * t / p)`
- **The Result:** A dynamic, personalized learning path that changes every day.
